Speaker: Daniel Kiper, Ross Philipson, Patrick Colp and Brian Payne (Oracle)

Oracle has been an early adopter of the TrenchBoot project and has been actively working to productize this technology. One of the key requirements of any shippable product is the ability to do an in-field upgrade. In this presentation, members of the Oracle TrenchBoot team will discuss their changes to grub, the Linux kernel and u-root, present a solution for performing upgrades, and provide the current development status.
